Web Design

At Born Digital, we are passionate about creating beautiful and functional design which is aligned with your brand and reflects your businesses core values. 

Every website we make aims for the highest standards – in particular, we rate the following as the most important factors: 

  • optimised for usability – websites that give a great user experience
  • optimised for compliance – standards based web design
  • optimise for search engines
  • optimise for users: snappy copy, speed and accessibility


Here at Born Digital, we follow a stepwise path to web design success. 

  1. Project Exploration – Creative Brief / Market and Competitor Exploration
  2. Content Maps and Information Architecture
  3. Wireframes Prototypes (see image 1)
  4. Visual Mockups (see image 2)
  5. Conversion to Web Formats
  6. Content & Functionality


Although this process varies with project and budget, its a great approach to ensure we have an accurate understanding of your needs.
